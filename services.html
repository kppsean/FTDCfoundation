SERVICES

Our student-based services are offered to local non-profit organizations in need of design work. The services our program offers include the following:



Print Design
Video & Motion Graphics
Web Design

We provide each client with a group of students containing the required skills to complete each project to satisfaction and on-time. Student teams will work directly with the client for an 11 week period (one trimester) to complete the assigned task. Select work will require an extended time-period of two trimesters and therefore will require 22 weeks. If your non-profit fits within these parameters please feel free to apply here.
